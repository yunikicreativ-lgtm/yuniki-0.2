# How to Demo Project Sentinel

To successfully demonstrate this project to buyers, follow this workflow to show off its most advanced features.

## 1. The Entrance (Trust & Encryption)
- **Action:** Open the app to the **Login Screen**.
- **Talking Point:** "Notice the 'Operator ID' and military-grade aesthetic. Sentinel is built on the principle of biometric encryption. It doesn't just feel like an app; it's a security terminal for your life."

## 2. The Dashboard (Proactive AI)
- **Action:** Tap 'Initialize System' and show the **Dashboard**.
- **Talking Point:** "Most safety apps are reactive—you have to press a button. Sentinel is proactive. See the 'AI Scanning' radar? It's currently using on-device neural networks to listen for distress sounds like glass breaking or screams. If something happens and you can't reach your phone, Sentinel already knows."
- **Feature to Highlight:** Point out the **Heart Rate** and **Mesh Node** status. "Even without internet, the Mesh network ensures your SOS reaches help by bouncing signals between other Sentinel devices."

## 3. The Ghost Protocol (Silent Protection)
- **Action:** Describe the volume-button pattern (5 taps).
- **Talking Point:** "If you're in a dangerous situation where you can't be seen using your phone, you trigger the **Ghost Protocol**. The screen goes black, looking like it's powered off, but it's actually streaming high-fidelity audio and GPS to our secure cloud."

## 4. Safety Heatmap (Real-time Intel)
- **Action:** Switch to the **Safety Map**.
- **Talking Point:** "We integrate real-time crime data and street lighting intel. The red zones show high-risk areas. The app won't just tell you where you are; it will suggest the 'Safest Route' home, not just the fastest."

## 5. Technical Superiority (The Sale)
- **Talking Point:** "The entire backend is built on Firebase and Node.js for zero-latency response. This isn't just a prototype; it's a scalable emergency infrastructure."

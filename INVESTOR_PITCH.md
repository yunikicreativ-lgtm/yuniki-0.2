# INVESTOR PITCH: Sentinel Global Security

## The Problem
Personal safety apps today are reactive. They require a user to have their phone in hand, unlocked, and the ability to press a button during a traumatic event. In 80% of violent crimes, this is impossible.

## The Solution: Sentinel
Sentinel is the world's first **Proactive AI Security Ecosystem**. It doesn't wait for you; it watches for you.

### 1. The Innovation (The "Wow" Factor)
- **Acoustic AI:** Real-time on-device audio classification (Screams, Gunshots, Glass break).
- **Omen LiDAR:** Uses hardware depth sensors to detect movement in the dark, effectively giving the user "eyes in the back of their head."
- **Aegis Vault:** Blockchain-verified evidence hashing. We don't just record crime; we make it legally indisputable.

### 2. Market Strategy (The "Money")
- **B2C (Subscription):** $9.99/mo for the "Guardian" tier (AI + Ghost Protocol).
- **B2B (Enterprise):** Licensing to Fortune 500 companies to protect traveling executives.
- **B2G (Government):** "Mesh Relay" integration for city-wide disaster response where cell towers are down.

### 3. Revenue Projections
- **Year 1:** Focus on high-crime metropolitan markets (Cape Town, Chicago, London).
- **Year 2:** Integration with smart wearables (Apple Watch, Garmin).
- **Exit Strategy:** Acquisition by major tech security players (ADT, Apple, or Google).
